# Integrative Thinking

Hold multiple models or frameworks simultaneously without forcing premature choice. Find creative resolutions that capture the advantages of multiple approaches.

## When to use
- Multiple valid approaches exist and choosing one loses value from others
- Need to combine insights from different domains or perspectives
- Simple either/or framing feels inadequate

## Guiding questions
1. What are the competing models or approaches?
2. What does each one capture that the others miss?
3. What would be lost by choosing just one?
4. Is there a creative resolution that preserves the advantages of multiple approaches?
5. What tensions remain even in the integrated solution?

## Output structure
- Models/approaches enumerated
- Unique value of each
- Integration attempt
- Remaining tensions
